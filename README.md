# 🌿 The Crossroads

> *Every choice leads somewhere. Where will your story go?*

The Crossroads is an interactive story engine that lets users craft and explore branching narratives — part game, part creative tool, entirely yours.

---

## What It Is

The Crossroads lets you build stories where every decision matters. Writers create paths, readers choose them. No two playthroughs are the same.

---

## Features

- 📖 **Branching story builder** — create narrative trees with multiple paths and outcomes
- 🔀 **Choice-driven gameplay** — readers make decisions that shape the story
- ✍️ **User-generated content** — anyone can write, share, and explore stories
- 🗺️ **Visual story map** *(planned)* — see your narrative branches laid out as a living map
